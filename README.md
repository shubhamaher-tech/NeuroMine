# 🧠 NeuroMine – AI-Powered Sustainability Platform

NeuroMine is an AI-powered **Life Cycle Assessment (LCA)** and **Circular Economy** platform designed for the **Metallurgy and Mining Industry**. It helps industries analyze environmental impact, evaluate circularity, and compare sustainable operational strategies using interactive dashboards and AI-driven insights.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

Mining and metallurgical industries generate significant environmental impacts such as carbon emissions, energy consumption, and waste generation. NeuroMine provides an intelligent decision-support platform that enables industries to:

- Predict environmental impact
- Assess circularity performance
- Compare multiple sustainability scenarios
- Generate AI-based recommendations for greener operations

---

## ✨ Features

### 🔮 AI Impact Prediction
- Predict CO₂ emissions
- Estimate operational cost
- Environmental risk analysis
- AI-generated sustainability recommendations
- Interactive trend visualization

### ♻️ Circularity Assessment
- Circularity score calculation
- Recycling and recovery analysis
- Material utilization visualization
- Improvement suggestions
- Interactive doughnut charts

### 🎯 Scenario Simulation
Compare different sustainability strategies:
- Current Operations
- Renewable Energy Adoption
- Process Efficiency Boost
- Circular Economy Practices
- Hybrid Sustainable Strategy

Outputs include:
- CO₂ emissions
- Energy consumption
- Operational cost
- Estimated annual savings

### 🤖 AI Assistant
A built-in virtual assistant helps users understand:
- Carbon reduction strategies
- Circular economy concepts
- Sustainability recommendations
- Scenario comparisons
